Skip the forty-field task manager. Each morning, drop what's on your plate into three simple groups:

Urgent

Things with teeth. If they slip today, someone feels it.

Progress

The work that compounds — projects, skills, the long game.

Extras

How it works

  • Open to today

    Whether you planned yesterday or took two weeks off, you land on a clean page. Older unfinished tasks wait in a quiet "Earlier" tray — review them when you're ready, or never.

  • Sort, then block time

    Give each task a bucket, then drag the important ones onto the day's timeline so they own a real slot instead of a wish.

  • Close the app

    Planning is five minutes, not a hobby. No streaks to keep alive, no red badges when life happens.
